About the work

A short paragraph. Enough for a curator; not so much it feels like marketing.

I’m a painter living and working in New York City, currently completing my MFA. My work moves between abstraction and interior space—edges, thresholds, and the way light sits on a wall or in the corner of a room. I think of the paintings as quiet, concentrated situations. The surfaces are built slowly, often over months, with attention to the way color can feel both sharp and hazy at once. This site is kept intentionally focused: a small group of recent works, clear information for collectors, and a direct way to reach me.
